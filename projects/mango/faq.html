F.A.Q

Q1 : What is exactly Mango, and what does it do ?
Mango is a cross-platform web browser based on Webkit, the most powerfull web engine ! It's fast and safe.
Q2 : What do I need to give Mango a try ?
It's worth it. You can choose any operating system you want. If you don't like it, just tell us why, we're here to listen !
Q3 : Who created Mango, and why ?
My name is Thomas Keunebroek, and I created this little browser for a project, while I was studying in Braunschweig (Germany). Please tell me what you think !
